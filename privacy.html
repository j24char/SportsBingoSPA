This Privacy Policy describes how Sports Bingo 2026 ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our iPhone application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Sports Bingo 2026:

Information you provide directly:

  • Messages or feedback you send to us through support channels

Information collected automatically:

  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • iOS version and device model for compatibility purposes

We do not collect any location data, contacts, photos, microphone input, or any biometric data. We never sell your personal information.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the App and its features
  • Analyze usage trends to improve gameplay and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements or build advertising profiles.

3 Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Multiplayer features: Your chosen display name and score may be visible to other players in a game session
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights

4 Data Retention

Anonymous usage and analytics data may be retained for up to 24 months to support product improvements.

5 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Advertising ID: You can reset or limit use of the Advertising Identifier in your iPhone's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address listed in Section 10.

6 Children's Privacy

Sports Bingo 2026 is intended for users aged 4 and older and is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately so we can delete that information.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by in-app notification.

Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
